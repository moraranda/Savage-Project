SAVAGE PROJECT
IT'S A PREMIUM URBAN CLOTHING BRAND
FOUNDED BY A COUPLE WHO WANTED TO CREATE SOMETHING DIFFERENT.

We are more than just a clothing brand. We are an expression of style and individuality, enthusiasts of urban culture. From our humble beginnings, we have strived to create clothing that reflects the essence of urban life. Every project we create is infused with the energy, diversity, and creativity of the streets that inspire us. At Savage Project, we endeavor to challenge conventions and redefine the standards of streetwear. Our collection is designed for those unafraid to stand out, for those who value their uniqueness, and for those who carry the spirit of the city wherever they go. But beyond fashion, we are also committed to the community around us.
